Our Services

What We Offer at Phil's Farms

Commercial Layer Poultry Farming

We specialize in raising layer birds for high-quality egg production, ensuring a steady supply of nutritious eggs to meet consumer demand across the Southeast.

Wholesale & Retail Egg Supply

We provide fresh, farm-produced eggs in bulk or retail quantities to businesses, supermarkets, and individuals at competitive prices.

Farm Automation

Our newly completed hatchery produces high-quality catfish, supplying fresh fish to markets and restaurants across the region.
